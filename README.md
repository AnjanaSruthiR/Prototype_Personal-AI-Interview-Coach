<h2>ReadyAI — Your Personal AI Interview Coach</h2>

<p>
  <strong>ReadyAI</strong> is an innovative AI-powered platform meticulously designed for comprehensive interview preparation. 
  It leverages structured self-assessment techniques to provide unparalleled feedback, ensuring users are thoroughly prepared for their next big opportunity.
</p>

<hr />

<h2>🎯 Project Overview</h2>

<h3>The Problem</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Job seekers, especially students and early-career professionals, lack access to consistent, affordable, and objective coaching.</li>
  <li>Professional interview coaching often costs between $100–$300 per hour, which is inaccessible for most students.</li>
  <li>Candidates struggle to objectively evaluate their own answers, detect filler words, or gauge clarity and confidence.</li>
  <li>Most existing platforms offer only general questions, failing to provide crucial company-specific context.</li>
</ul>

<h3>The Solution</h3>
<ul>
  <li>ReadyAI delivers personalized, company-specific interview coaching.</li>
  <li>Provides objective scoring across four key dimensions: Technical, Behavioral, Confidence, and Clarity.</li>
  <li>Supplies actionable weekly improvement roadmaps designed for continuous growth.</li>
</ul>

<hr />

<h2>✨ Key Features</h2>

<h3>1. AI Answer Analyzer</h3>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Filler Word Detection:</strong> Advanced NLP models detect specific filler words such as "um," "like," and "you know".</li>
  <li><strong>STAR Scoring:</strong> Analyzes text to score Situation, Task, Action, and Result completeness out of 100.</li>
  <li><strong>Intelligent Rewriting:</strong> Generates rewritten, improved versions of answers for user comparison and practical improvement.</li>
</ul>

<h3>2. Live Mock & Gesture Analysis</h3>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Real-time Computer Vision:</strong> Analyzes live video feeds to score eye contact, posture, facial expressions, and hand gestures.</li>
  <li><strong>Body Language Coaching:</strong> Reveals critical habits often invisible during solo practice, such as avoiding crossing arms.</li>
  <li><strong>Live AI Tips:</strong> Displays guidance during the interview to help users maintain STAR structure and enhance delivery.</li>
</ul>

<h3>3. Personalized Roadmaps</h3>
<ul>
  <li><strong>4-Week Plan:</strong> Builds a structured journey from Foundations to Final Readiness.</li>
  <li><strong>Adaptive Tasks:</strong> Includes daily practice tasks like self-assessment quizzes and full timed mock interviews.</li>
  <li><strong>Gamification:</strong> Features streak alerts and progress dashboards to foster ongoing development.</li>
</ul>

<h3>4. Company-Specific Preparation</h3>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Industry Patterns:</strong> Tailored question banks for major companies like Google, Amazon, Meta, and Microsoft.</li>
  <li><strong>Targeted Rounds:</strong> Supports prep for Technical, Behavioral, System Design, and HR rounds.</li>
</ul>
